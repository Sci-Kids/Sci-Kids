About this page

Sci-Kids is a website that has the purpose of helping elementary students to improve their knowledge in science. Every lesson is summarized in a way that it is easy to comprehend, and there is also a fun video included to review all the lessons provided. Furthermore, there is an activity available to put your knowledge and what you learned to the test.
